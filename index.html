Frequently Asked Questions

What is the meaning of the word "Noveracion"?

"Noveracion" is a term we created that embodies our innovative philosophy. "NOV" is inspired by the Italian word "novus," meaning "to create something new." "VER" signifies convergence, and "ACION" is derived from the Spanish "innovación." Together, they reflect our mission to harness innovative thinking and cutting-edge technologies to create sustainable value for our clients.

How does Noveracion Global stay ahead in the rapidly evolving technology landscape?

We maintain a strong grasp of technology fundamentals, enabling us to quickly adapt and deliver innovative solutions. Our hands-on learning framework and collaboration with industry experts ensure our strategies are informed by the latest insights, allowing us to address complex challenges effectively.

Is there a niche that you specialize in and how can they benefit our organization?

We offer comprehensive contact center automation solutions designed to enhance efficiency, reduce operational costs, and improve customer experience. By leveraging GenAI technologies, we can help automate routine tasks, streamline workflows, and provide insights that enable your teams to focus on high-value interactions, ultimately driving customer satisfaction and loyalty.

What sets your approach to innovation apart from traditional consulting firms?

We prioritize collaboration and innovation. Unlike traditional firms, we focus solely on innovation consulting using advanced technologies like GenAI. Our unique Jugaad approach optimizes resources, while our proprietary Innovation Assessment Framework ensures our solutions are closely aligned with your business goals.

Can you explain your Innovation Assessment Framework?

Our framework helps organizations identify and capitalize on hidden opportunities while minimizing risks. It evaluates various parameters to assess potential ROI and time to capital recovery, culminating in a feasibility report that outlines actionable implementations to maximize value.

How does Noveracion Global balance innovation and cost-effectiveness?

We focus on delivering profitable results, ensuring that our solutions yield positive ROI. If a proposed feature lacks value, we transparently recommend alternatives that align better with your strategic objectives.

What measurable impact can clients expect from your solutions?

Clients can expect tangible results, such as increased revenue and improved customer satisfaction. Every initiative is aligned with your goals, driving meaningful improvements. For community-focused projects, we also emphasize long-term environmental and social benefits.

What specific industries do you specialize in, and how does your expertise translate to our business challenges?

How do you ensure that your innovation solutions align with our existing business strategies?

We conduct a thorough analysis of your current strategies and collaborate with key stakeholders to ensure our solutions enhance your business objectives, driving meaningful outcomes.

What methodologies do you use to assess and prioritize innovation opportunities?

Our proprietary Innovation Assessment Framework employs a range of metrics to identify and prioritize opportunities based on impact and feasibility, ensuring we focus on high-value projects.

How do you measure the success of your innovation initiatives?

Success is defined through tailored KPIs, such as ROI, customer satisfaction, and process efficiency. We establish relevant metrics with you upfront to ensure transparency and track progress effectively.

What is your approach to change management and technology adoption?

We foster a collaborative change management strategy, involving your teams throughout implementation. We provide comprehensive training and support to empower employees to leverage new technologies confidently.

How do you address potential risks when implementing innovative solutions?

We conduct comprehensive risk assessments during planning to identify challenges and develop mitigation strategies. Our iterative approach allows us to continuously evaluate risks and adjust as necessary.

Can you describe your teams qualifications in delivering GenAI solutions?
